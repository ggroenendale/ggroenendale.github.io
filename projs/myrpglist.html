    My RPG List

    Summary

    The idea behind this project was to create a platform where Table Top Role Players can share the stories they are telling with their favorite game systems and fans can find other stories their favorite players are connected to.

    Problem

    • Content Creators and players are often at mercy of the algorithms in YouTube, Twitch, Instagram, and TikTok for getting their content out.
    • Players often play in one campaign without viewers knowing where else they can find their other appearances.
    • Game Designers, Module Writers, Artists also have to fight the algorithms for getting their creations into the spotlight. There are many more game systems than D&D and Pathfinder.
    • Viewers have a hard time finding characters and stories they most associate with. Western themes, characters with similar gender identities and sexuality's, certain species or races and character classes.

    Requirements

    1. A frontend view for each type of content - Campaign, Character, Game System, Actor, etc.
    2. Users are able to login to the platform
      1. Some users will have ownership over some content they are assigned to
