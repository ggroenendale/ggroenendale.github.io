    Here is some of the work I have done.

    Build a Bar Crawl

    This is an ongoing project that began in my advanced multimedia authoring class. The goal was to create a web app that will help you plan the best bar crawl possible based on a starting location like a house, restaurant, or lounge. The app connects to the Yelp API and pulls as many pubs, bars, and breweries nearby and pins them on a map for you to select your picks.

    Follow the link to try it out!

    Data Management - Final Project

    Data management was a class that went into detail on the functions of a Microsoft SQL Server database as well as how those functions could help a business manage their data more efficiently. This semester long project required us to create an operational database for a fictitious organization. The database would have to meet several requirements and be a database that produces value for the organization. I chose to make a database based around a project management office doing primarily construction. This document details my final findings and some of my operational queries and procedures.

    E-Commerce - Final Project

    As group projects go this was one of my favorite projects and one that I was very excited to lead. The project required us to invent a business that sells a product online via PrestaShop. We built a revenue model and marketing strategy as well as wrote out a detailed competitive analysis and operational details. We also created a product line. This document details our final findings and strategy.
